Project Overview

Pickle House is a product showcase and e-commerce site for a homemade pickle brand. The goal was to create a warm, appetising online storefront that highlights their range of artisan pickles and makes it easy for customers to browse and place orders.

The client needed a simple yet professional website to move beyond WhatsApp-only sales and establish a real online brand presence with a custom domain and a product catalogue.

Timeline 1 Week
Tech Stack HTML, CSS, JavaScript
Client Artisan Pickle Brand
Type Product Showcase / E-commerce

Business Impact

Online Storefront

Moved the business from informal WhatsApp orders to a professional online store customers can browse any time.

Product Catalogue

All products are showcased with descriptions, variants, and pricing — reducing back-and-forth queries from customers.

SEO & Discoverability

Proper meta tags, semantic HTML, and a clean URL structure make the brand findable on Google searches.

Mobile-First Design

Fully responsive layout ensures a great shopping experience on phones, where most customers browse.
