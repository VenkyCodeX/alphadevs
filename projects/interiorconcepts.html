Project Overview

Gauri Interiors is a professional interior design firm that needed a modern, visually rich website to showcase their portfolio of residential and commercial projects. The goal was to create a premium digital presence that reflects the quality and elegance of their work.

The website features a curated project gallery, detailed service pages, a client testimonials section, and a contact/inquiry form — all designed to convert visitors into leads.

The site is currently in active development with the final design and content being finalized with the client.

Key Features

Project Gallery

A visually rich gallery showcasing residential and commercial interior design projects with high-quality photography.

Services Showcase

Detailed service pages covering living room design, bedroom interiors, kitchen remodels, and commercial spaces.

Fully Responsive

Pixel-perfect design across all devices — desktop, tablet, and mobile — ensuring a seamless browsing experience.

Inquiry Form

A clean contact and project inquiry form that lets potential clients describe their requirements and get a callback.

Client Testimonials

A testimonials section featuring real client reviews to build trust and credibility with new visitors.

SEO Optimized

Built with SEO best practices — meta tags, semantic HTML, fast load times, and proper heading structure.

Development Process

01 — Discovery

Understood the client's brand identity, target audience, and the types of interior projects they specialize in.

02 — Design

Created wireframes and high-fidelity mockups in Figma, focusing on a warm, premium aesthetic that matches interior design sensibilities.

03 — Development

Built the site with clean HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no heavy frameworks, ensuring fast load times and easy maintenance.

04 — Deployment

Deployed on Vercel for instant global CDN delivery, automatic HTTPS, and zero-downtime updates.
