Project Overview

BikeRentalHub is a multi-vendor platform that allows bike rental businesses to list their fleet online and accept bookings digitally. The platform was built to solve the problem of offline-only bike rentals in Hyderabad, where customers had no way to check availability or book in advance.

The client needed a professional online presence that could handle multiple vendors, showcase different bike models, and allow customers to book directly through the website.

Timeline 2 Weeks
Tech Stack HTML, CSS, JavaScript
Client Bike Rental Business, Hyderabad
Type Multi-vendor Platform

Business Impact

Online Presence

The business moved from zero online presence to a fully functional website, making it discoverable on Google.

Online Bookings

Customers can now check availability and book bikes directly through the website, reducing phone call dependency.

Multi-vendor Support

Multiple rental vendors can list their fleet on a single platform, increasing inventory and customer choice.

Business Growth

Professional website helped the business attract more customers and establish credibility in the local market.
