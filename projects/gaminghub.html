Project Overview

Gaming Rental Hub is an online platform that allows gamers to rent consoles, controllers, and gaming accessories without buying them. The platform targets students and casual gamers who want to experience gaming without the high upfront cost.

The website was built with a modern gaming aesthetic, easy browsing experience, and a simple booking flow to maximize conversions.

Business Impact

New Market

Created an entirely new online rental market for gaming gear — first of its kind in the local area.

Online Bookings

Customers can browse available gear and book online, making the rental process seamless and fast.

Target Audience

Attracts students and casual gamers who want to try gaming without investing in expensive hardware.

Revenue Growth

Online presence enabled the business to reach customers beyond word-of-mouth and grow consistently.
